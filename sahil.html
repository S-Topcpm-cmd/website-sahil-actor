Omar Sahil

Omar Sahil is a young and talented actor with a natural gift for performance and storytelling. Originally from Afghanistan and now based in Germany, Omar brings passion, energy, and emotional depth to every role he takes on. His ability to express powerful emotions and connect with the audience makes him stand out, even at the beginning of his acting journey.

Personality

Fluent in German, English, and Farsi, Omar brings a diverse cultural background to his work, allowing him to portray a wide range of characters. While continuing his education, he is fully dedicated to growing as an actor and taking on new challenges, whether in front of the camera or on stage.

Life and career

In 2012, Ulusoy shot a short film, Paranoia. From 2013 until 2015, he played the lead role Yaman Koper in Medcezir (Tide in English), loosely based on The O.C. His character, Yaman, was based on Ben McKenzie’s character Ryan Atwood.

From 2016 until 2017, Ulusoy starred in the TV series İçerde alongside Aras Bulut Iynemli and Bensu Soral — the show was partially based on the movie The Departed.
